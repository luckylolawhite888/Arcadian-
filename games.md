# 🎮 Games List

Interactive button-based games created for Maya.

## Music Quiz (Created: 2026-03-24)

**Format:** 5-question multiple choice quiz
**Categories:** Music, artists, albums, hits
**Mechanics:**
- All questions displayed at once
- Button-based answers
- Silent answer tracking
- Final score reveal

**Questions from first game:**
1. Which artist released the 2023 hit "Flowers"?
   - ✅ Miley Cyrus (correct)
   - ❌ Taylor Swift
   - ❌ Beyoncé
   - ❌ Dua Lipa

2. Which K-pop group's 2023 song "Cupid" went viral on TikTok?
   - ❌ BTS
   - ❌ BLACKPINK
   - ✅ FIFTY FIFTY (correct)
   - ❌ NewJeans

3. Which artist's 2024 album "The Tortured Poets Department" broke records?
   - ✅ Taylor Swift (correct)
   - ❌ Olivia Rodrigo
   - ❌ Billie Eilish
   - ❌ Lana Del Rey

4. Which 2025 Drake & 21 Savage collab was their third joint project?
   - ❌ Her Loss
   - ❌ What A Time
   - ❓ Certified Lover Boy (answer pending)
   - ❌ For All The Dogs

5. Who won Album of the Year at the 2026 Grammys?
   - ❌ SZA - SOS
   - ❌ Beyoncé - Renaissance
   - ❓ Taylor Swift - Midnights (answer pending)
   - ❌ Harry Styles - Harry's House

**Game Flow:**
1. Display all questions with buttons
2. User clicks answers
3. Track silently
4. User says "DONE"
5. Show score & results
