# 🏠 Kingsly Group — Full Feature Proposal

## What We're Building

A complete business operating system for Kingsly Group, combining **CRM + AI Assistant + Social Media Automation** into one seamless system. Everything works together.

---

## 🧩 Part 1: CRM & Client Management

**What it does:** Replaces spreadsheets, sticky notes, and missed follow-ups.

- **Sales Pipeline** — Track every buyer from enquiry → viewing → offer → completion
- **Lettings Pipeline** — Manage landlords, tenants, renewals, inspections
- **Contact Management** — All clients, properties, and notes in one place
- **Automated Follow-ups** — No more "sorry, I forgot to call you back"
- **Property Database** — Every listing with status, price history, interested buyers
- **Activity Log** — See every call, email, and note per client
- **Pipeline Dashboard** — See exactly where every deal is at a glance

**Why Ali needs it:** Estate agents live and die by follow-ups. This never drops a ball.

---

## 🤖 Part 2: AI Personal Assistant

**What it does:** Handles the repeat work so Ali can focus on closing deals.

- **AI Property Enquiry Handler** — When someone messages about a listing, the AI responds instantly with details, availability, and books a viewing
- **AI Viewing Scheduler** — Coordinates between buyer and seller availability automatically
- **AI Valuation Booking** — Captures property valuation requests and puts them straight on the calendar
- **AI FAQ Responder** — Answers common questions (fees, process, areas) without Ali typing a word
- **AI Follow-up Engine** — Checks in with leads who went cold, tenants approaching renewal, landlords due a check-in
- **AI Market Update Sender** — Sends personalised market reports to past clients automatically

**The magic:** Ali wakes up to a dashboard that says "3 new enquiries handled, 2 viewings booked, 1 valuation confirmed" — all done while he slept.

---

## 📱 Part 3: Multi-WhatsApp System

**What it does:** Multiple phone numbers, one device, full control.

- **Personal WhatsApp** — Ali's personal number
- **Business WhatsApp** — Kingsly's office number
- **Separate chat lists** per number, never mix them up
- **Send & receive** from any number in one app
- **Monitor mode** — I check both numbers for missed messages and alert Ali
- **Reply on command** — Ali says "Lola, reply to Mr Jones saying Thursday at 2pm works" — done

**Why it's a game-changer:** Normal phones only support one WhatsApp. This gives Ali as many as he needs.

---

## 📣 Part 4: Social Media Automation

**What it does:** Keeps Kingsly visible without Ali spending hours posting.

- **Auto Property Posts** — When a new listing goes live, it's automatically posted to:
  - Facebook (page + local groups)
  - Instagram (with property photos)
  - Twitter/X
- **Auto "Just Sold" / "Let Agreed" Announcements** — Celebrating wins builds trust
- **Weekly Market Update** — Auto-generated post with local stats
- **Review Collector** — Sends happy clients a review request → posts positive reviews automatically
- **Content Calendar** — Pre-scheduled posts for weeks at a time
- **DM Auto-Reply** — When someone messages on social media asking about a property, the AI responds and captures their details into the CRM

**The result:** Kingsly looks like a marketing powerhouse posting daily — but it runs itself.

---

## 🔄 How It All Connects

```
Social Media ──→ AI captures leads ──→ CRM stores them ──→ AI follows up
                                    │
                                    └──→ WhatsApp notification to Ali
                                    └──→ Viewing automatically booked
                                    └──→ Pipeline updated
```

Everything feeds into everything else. A Facebook comment about a property becomes a CRM lead, gets followed up via WhatsApp, and a viewing gets booked — without Ali touching a button.
