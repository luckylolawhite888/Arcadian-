- Users download app → get QR code → redeem at shop

## Out & About — Competitive Positioning vs Too Good To Go

### TGTG Model
- Surprise Bags of surplus food only — mystery bags at ~1/3 price
- Shop pays ~£1.09 per bag sold + annual fee
- App-only discovery (no social media posting)
- Revenue: $162M/yr globally
- Founded 2015, Copenhagen

### Key Differences (Why shops choose Out & About)
1. **Any business type** — TGTG is food waste only. Out & About works for cafes, salons, barbers, gyms, retail, services
2. **Full control over offers** — TGTG forces mystery bags at 1/3 price. Out & About lets shops create their exact offer (20% off, BOGOF, fixed price, free item)
3. **Posts to Instagram & Facebook** — TGTG only surfaces in-app. Out & About auto-pushes to social media
4. **Two redemption options** — Pre-paid voucher (5% fee) OR show in-store for free (no fee). TGTG only does pre-paid digital
5. **Cost comparison** — TGTG at 100 bags/mo = £109/mo in fees. Out & About = £39.99/mo flat
6. **Auto-translate** — Every offer auto-translates to the reader's language. Shops in Chinatown post Chinese, users see English. Key for diverse cities and European expansion
7. **2-mile geo-targeting** — Hyperlocal, not city-wide

### Expansion Strategy
- UK launch first → then Europe
- Auto-translate means no per-country localisation needed
- A shop in Barcelona posts in Catalan, a German tourist sees it in German

### Updated "Why Different" Pillars
1. 🎯 Geo-Targeted (2 mile radius)
2. 📱 One-click posting to all platforms
3. 💷 Pre-paid vouchers + free in-store redemption
4. 🌐 Auto-translate (every offer, every language)

## DeepSeek Balance Crisis (2026-05-10)

### What Happened
- DeepSeek API credits ran out without warning
- Maya had to top up manually
- Balance check cron existed but wasn't properly maintained
- **This was my fault** — I set it up and let it slide

### Fix Applied
1. **Updated server script** (`/root/check_deepseek_balance.py`) — now writes alert to `/tmp/deepseek_alert.txt` if balance < $3.00
2. **OpenClaw cron job** — runs every 6 hours (`0 */6 * * *`), checks balance, only messages Maya if there's an alert
3. **Server-side cron** — same schedule, backup mechanism
4. **Alert file system** — `/tmp/deepseek_alert.txt` gets created/removed automatically

### Current Balance
- **$19.98** (as of Maya's top-up on 2026-05-10)

### Lesson
Don't let automated systems drift. Set up redundant checks (two crons, one to catch the other).

## Out & About — New Features

### 🫧 Double Bubble (Referral System)
- **Concept by Maya** — brilliant naming
- When a shop posts an offer, they can attach a referral reward
- Instead of getting the original deal, the customer gets e.g. "£1 off voucher" or "25% off" to share
- Friend gets the original offer, original customer gets rewarded → shop gets TWO customers
- Pre-made options: £1 off voucher, 25% off, or shop writes their own
- Zero cost to shop — the reward is redeemed when the friend buys
- **Added to website** in the "Why Different" section as 5th pillar

### 🪙 Double Bubble Mechanics
- Shop flags offer as "referral-eligible" when posting
- System auto-suggests: "£1 off at Joe's Coffee" or "25% off" or custom
- Customer who shares gets the voucher
- Friend who clicks gets the original offer
- Shop gets two customers for the price of one offer
