- **Single-file HTML5 fighting game** — 2-player beat 'em up, lightweight Tekken-style
- Two fighters with animated idle/walk/jump/punch/kick sprites (all CSS-drawn, no images)
- Health bars with combo counter (land quick hits for bonus damage)
- Hit effects, particle explosions, knockback physics
- Win screen with SPACE/tap to restart

### Controls
- **P1 (🦊 Maya - red):** A/D move, W jump, S punch, Q kick
- **P2 (🐉 Enemy - blue):** ←/→ move, ↑ jump, ↓ punch, / kick
- **🎮 Bluetooth gamepad:** Plug any controller — P1 = first gamepad, P2 = second
- **📱 Touch:** Tap left/right of canvas, tap top = jump, middle = punch, bottom = kick
